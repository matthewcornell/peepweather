What is RC Weather?

RC Weather is a web application that shows a color-coded stoplight view of the weather for the next seven days, hour-by-hour. In one glance you can quickly see what the flying looks like for the week, and plan accordingly.

How it works »

Who is it for?

From park fliers to 500 class helis to giant-scale warbirds, all RC pilots need to know the weather to decide when is good for flying. But we're busy and most forecasts take time to get what we really care about: a go/no go for each hour.

Why use it?

Unlike other weather sites that either provide too little hourly information or way too much, RC Weather makes it easy for you to tell, in one look, how the week is stacking up. Lots of red? Relax and hit the sim. Tons of green? Call a buddy and meet at the field!
