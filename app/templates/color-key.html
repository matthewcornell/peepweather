Color calculation

Each hour's color is determined by evaluating each weather parameter individually to get one of three desirability ratings - Low, Medium, and High - based on simple equation. Here's a nerdy description of how that's decided, based on ranges: 
    precip: [0, 10]: High   [11, 30]: Medium  [31, ...]: Low
    temp:   [..., 32]: Low  [33, 41]: Medium  [42, 70]: High  [71, 85]: Medium    [86, ...]: Low
    wind:   [0,  8]: High   [ 9, 12]: Medium  [13, ...]: Low
